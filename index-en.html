About Us

Building bridges for global youth innovation since 2024

Founded in 2024, Boundless Youth Forum is a diverse platform for global youth innovation and entrepreneurship, jointly initiated by young entrepreneurs, scientists, and industry elites. Through organizing in-depth online and offline exchange activities, we connect resources, inspire ideas, and build an open and inclusive international entrepreneurial community.

We have successfully organized overseas competitions for Wuzhen Cup, Jinhang Cup and other entrepreneurship competitions, roundtable discussions and return visits, and conducted in-depth interviews with numerous entrepreneurs, investors, and corporate executives to promote deep cooperation between Chinese and foreign youth in entrepreneurship.

Our Team

100+ global innovators across AI, healthcare, fintech, and creative industries

Currently, the forum has 100+ core members from around the world, covering cutting-edge fields including artificial intelligence, healthcare, cultural creativity, and fintech. Members and community partners include several star startups valued at over $10 million, such as Convergence AI, Ridescan, and Camel AI.

Successful Startups

Multiple members have secured millions in funding, including successful selection for Zhejiang talent programs, achieving million-level revenue, and driving multiple technological innovations to market.

International Honors

Many members have won major international awards, including the Golden Rooster Award, St. Andrews Cross Award, Lumen Prize, and Red Dot Award.

Academic Contributions

Multiple members have published papers in top global journals including ICLR, IEEE, and ICML, with research cited over 1500 times on Google Scholar.
