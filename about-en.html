About Us

关于我们

Founded in 2024, Boundless Youth Forum is a diverse, high-quality exchange platform. The forum was jointly established by a group of entrepreneurs, young scientists, and industry professionals dedicated to youth development. We focus on three core areas: career support, research connections, and entrepreneurship assistance, helping global youth find direction, spark inspiration, and achieve breakthroughs in their careers, academics, and startups. Boundless Youth Forum is committed to breaking down geographical, industry, and disciplinary boundaries, building a bridge connecting top resources with outstanding youth. Through high-quality forum activities, precise resource matching, and practical professional support, we provide a platform for global youth growth and collaboration.

Since its establishment, Boundless Youth Forum has established partnerships with numerous top institutions and industrial parks, including the British Academy of Science and Culture, Harvard China Venture Summit, and Haining Juanhu Science City. Our founding members and partners come from multiple cutting-edge fields including artificial intelligence, healthcare, creative culture, and fintech, jointly promoting the integration of career development and technological innovation. Boundless Youth Forum is not just a platform for exchange, but a stage where dreams set sail. Here, you will meet like-minded partners, gain inspiration, resources, and opportunities to explore infinite possibilities together. Boundless Youth believes that every young person has the ability to change the world with wisdom and action, achieving a better future.
