About Us

关于我们

Founded in 2024, Boundless Youth Forum is a diverse platform dedicated to global youth innovation and entrepreneurship, jointly initiated by young entrepreneurs, scientists, and industry elites. Through organizing in-depth online and offline exchange activities, the forum connects resources, inspires ideas, and builds an open and inclusive international entrepreneurial community.

We have successfully organized entrepreneurship competitions such as the Wuzhen Cup Overseas Division and Jinhang Cup, roundtable discussions, and return visits, conducting in-depth interviews with numerous entrepreneurs, investors, and corporate executives to promote deep cooperation between Chinese and international youth on their entrepreneurial journey.

Investment Institutions

In early and later-stage funding, the forum maintains close ties with renowned domestic and international investment institutions including local industrial funds, XBOT Incubator, Suzhou Yuanhe Holdings, Zhongjing Capital, Source Code Capital, Bilingxing Capital, Qingtong Capital, Antler, Square Peg Ventures, NextGen Ventures, Mendeley Ventures, and Pegasus VC, providing comprehensive funding and implementation support for youth entrepreneurship projects.

International Cooperation

The forum has established long-term cooperative relationships with globally renowned universities, innovation institutions, and industrial parks including Cambridge University, Imperial College London, Tsinghua UK, London Arts University Computing Research Centre, University of Queensland Ventures, Harvard Innovation Center, University of Pennsylvania Innovation Center, Haining Juanhu Science City, Huawei, Xiaohongshu, Alibaba Cloud, Unitree Technology, and Baidu.
