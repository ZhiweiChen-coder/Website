About Boundless Youth Forum

关于无界青年论坛

Mission

Boundless Youth Forum empowers global youth through cross-border advisory, events, and community. We bridge resources across industries, geographies, and disciplines so young founders, researchers, and professionals can grow faster together.

Approach

  • Founder-first — We center the needs and pace of founders and emerging leaders.
  • Evidence-based — We prioritize data, real outcomes, and measurable progress.
  • Open collaboration — We connect people and institutions to multiply impact.

Partners & Highlights

合作伙伴与成果亮点

Universities

Collaboration with global universities and research institutes supporting research projects and talent development.

Innovation Ecosystems

Connections to incubators, industrial parks, and capital to accelerate startup growth.

Global Community

Diverse members across AI, healthcare, finance, and creative industries in multiple countries.
