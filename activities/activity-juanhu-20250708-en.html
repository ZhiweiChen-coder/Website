Juanhu Summer Gathering for Returning Talents

2025 Overseas High-level Talent Return Innovation and Entrepreneurship Exchange · Visit Review

Date: July 8, 2025 Location: Haining, Zhejiang Tags: #Talent #Science Park #Haining #Visit

Event Summary

On July 8th, Boundless Youth Forum organizer Chen Zhiwei led a delegation to Juanhu International Science City in Haining, Zhejiang for exchange and consultation.

Team members included master's and doctoral students from world-renowned universities such as University of Manchester, Harvard University, University of Edinburgh, University of Tokyo, New York University, and University of Hong Kong.

The delegation learned about the overall planning and spatial layout of the science city, focusing on functional systems, industrial directions, and key platforms such as Zhejiang University International Campus and Science and Innovation Financial Center.

Boundless Youth Forum focuses on three core directions: career support, research connections, and entrepreneurship assistance, continuously connecting global top talents with industrial resources to help youth achieve diverse growth. Through such on-site visits, we not only learned about Haining's industrial ecosystem but also felt the local government's attention and support for talent.
