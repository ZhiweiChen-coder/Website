Boundless Youth Forum at GOSIM HANGZHOU 2025

Global Open-source Innovation Meetup · Hangzhou

Date: January 15, 2025 Location: Hangzhou, Zhejiang Tags: #Open Source #AI #GOSIM #Hangzhou

Event Summary

Boundless Youth Forum made its appearance at GOSIM HANGZHOU 2025. Founder Chen Zhiwei and multiple members participated as guests, engaging in deep exchanges with the global open source and AI community, sharing cutting-edge insights and promoting cross-border collaboration.

Forum members delivered presentations on learning mechanism reconstruction and AI ethics, receiving enthusiastic responses from the audience. The conference focused on open standards and open source innovation for next-generation AI, providing global perspectives and practical paths for the industry ecosystem.

In today's rapidly developing artificial intelligence landscape, we need to rethink the essence of learning and innovation. Shifting from traditional knowledge acquisition to creativity generation is not only personal growth but also the driving force for social progress. At the same time, discussions on AI ethics and regulation are crucial to ensure that technological development always serves human welfare.
