2025 "Juanhu Dream" Global Entrepreneurship Innovation Competition London Roadshow Successfully Held

30+ quality projects registered, 6 excellent projects shortlisted for the roadshow, 3 projects won "Excellence Innovation Award"

Date: August 28, 2025 Location: London, UK Tags: #Global Entrepreneurship #Roadshow #London #Juanhu Dream

Event Summary

On August 28, 2025, the "Juanhu Dream" Global Entrepreneurship Innovation Competition London Roadshow was successfully held in London.

This event was organized by the Haining International Cooperation Education Development Office and hosted by the Oxford Voyage Sino-British (Juanhu) Innovation Center, with co-organization by Zhejiang Tsinghua Yangtze River Delta Research Institute Haining International Innovation Center, Haining Eurasian Talent Innovation Center, Yangtze River Delta Global Science and Technology Innovation Roadshow Center, Xihe Science and Innovation, and Boundless Youth Forum (Shanghai Wujie Innovation). Distinguished guests including Zhang Quan, Party Committee Member and Deputy Director of Haining International Cooperation Education Development Office, and Hua Jing, Investment Vice President of Zhejiang Guanfu Private Fund, attended the event.

In his speech, Zhang Quan stated that Juanhu International Science City is integrating into the global innovation network with a more open attitude, continuously optimizing the talent development environment and industrial innovation ecosystem, and introduced Juanhu International Science City to the participating guests.

During the roadshow session, project representatives showcased their technical highlights, business models, and innovation advantages. Expert judges conducted in-depth questioning and evaluation from dimensions such as technical core and business value, with enthusiastic and thorough exchanges. After fierce competition, 3 projects were ultimately selected to receive the "Excellence Innovation Award".

In this competition, Boundless Youth Forum (Shanghai Wujie Innovation) participated deeply in preparation and organization as a co-organizer, actively leveraging its advantages in international youth innovation and entrepreneurship exchange and resource integration. Boundless Youth Forum is committed to promoting exchanges and cooperation between Chinese and foreign young entrepreneurs, helping quality innovation projects reach the international stage. Through this event, the forum further demonstrated its connectivity and empowerment value in the global innovation network, and will continue to contribute to the interaction and development of young talents between China and the UK.
