Our Activities

我们的活动

Boundless Youth Forum provides diverse activities for global youth and has gained widespread attention from both students and industry professionals. Our activities include:

High-Quality Themed Discussions:

We invite industry experts, young entrepreneurs, and seasoned scholars to engage in in-depth discussions on cutting-edge fields including artificial intelligence, fintech, healthcare, and creative industries. These discussions help youth understand the latest industry trends and future development opportunities from multiple perspectives.

Sharing Sessions:

The forum provides career skills enhancement and research & entrepreneurship sharing sessions, including resume optimization, interview coaching, career planning, as well as research methodology, business plan writing, and investment matching techniques. Members gain practical experience that can be directly applied, helping youth breakthrough academic and entrepreneurial bottlenecks.

Resource Matching and Collaboration:

The forum provides a cross-industry, cross-domain resource sharing platform, offering youth direct access to corporate mentors, research institutions, and venture capital funds. Through startup pitches and project showcases, we help youth promote their research achievements or entrepreneurial projects to attract potential investors and partners.

Social and Networking Events:

The forum organizes regular offline and online social activities, including roundtable discussions, themed gatherings, and cross-disciplinary salons, allowing youth to meet like-minded partners. We promote interdisciplinary interaction and collaboration, encouraging youth from different fields to explore future development possibilities together.

Innovation Competitions and Hackathons:

The forum hosts technological innovation competitions, entrepreneurship challenges, and hackathons sponsored by major universities and governments, providing youth with a platform for practical innovation. Participants can work in teams to solve real-world problems while receiving mentorship, investment opportunities, and substantial rewards.

International Exchange and Visits:

The forum provides members with opportunities to collaborate and exchange with overseas universities, tech companies, and international organizations, including overseas visits, exchange programs, and international seminars. This helps youth expand their global perspective, build international networks, and explore broader career and research possibilities.
