Jiankang Biotech Wins Second Prize in Haining Entrepreneurship Competition

Won second prize in the 13th 'Rising East, Winning in Haining' entrepreneurship competition

Date: September 17, 2025 Location: Haining, Zhejiang Tags: #Life Sciences #Competition #Haining

Event Summary

On September 17, 2025, at the final award ceremony of the 13th "Rising East, Winning in Haining" entrepreneurship competition, Jiankang Biotech, led by Boundless Youth Forum member Dr. Zhao Qiuchen, won the second prize with outstanding innovation capabilities.

Since its launch in March, this competition has attracted nearly 200 projects from around the world, with 65% led by PhD holders and 30% having overseas backgrounds. After rigorous screening and intense competition, 44 high-quality projects made it to the final stage, covering key areas such as semiconductors, artificial intelligence, life sciences, and intelligent manufacturing, highly aligned with Haining's "241" industrial layout.

On this passionate stage, Jiankang Biotech won unanimous recognition from judges and audiences with solid research capabilities and future-oriented industrial value. As one of the driving forces behind this, Boundless Youth Forum is committed to connecting domestic and international innovation forces, providing support for young entrepreneurs and research teams through competition connections, resource linking, and showcase platforms, helping them reach bigger stages. We firmly believe: working together will bring everyone a better future and achieve more dreams!
