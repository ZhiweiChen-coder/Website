Our Services

我们的服务

Since its establishment, Boundless Youth Forum has been committed to providing personalized services covering career development, research exploration, and entrepreneurial practice.

Whether you're a youth stepping into the workplace, a scholar dedicated to research breakthroughs, or an entrepreneur eager to realize creative ideas, you can find your suitable growth path here. Our services include:

Career Referrals:

  • Internal Referral Opportunities: Boundless Youth Forum has established long-term partnerships with numerous mature companies/startups across China, regularly providing abundant internal referral opportunities to help youth quickly enter their ideal positions.

  • Career Planning Consultation: Providing personalized career planning services, developing clear development paths based on personal interests, market demands, and career development trends.

  • Resume and Interview Coaching: Expert team provides resume optimization and interview skills coaching services to help improve job application success rates.

Research Connections:

  • Academic Collaboration Platform: Providing collaboration opportunities with domestic and international universities including Zhejiang University, University of Edinburgh, University of Cambridge, and research institutions, supporting research project cooperation and innovation.

  • Research Resource Matching: Helping connect research projects, finding mentors, partners, applying for research funding, and promoting research results transformation and application.

  • Mentor Guidance: Providing one-on-one research mentor coaching, offering research direction guidance, paper review, and other professional support.

Entrepreneurship Support:

  • Investment Matching: Through Boundless Youth Forum's resource network, participants have opportunities to receive support from government industry funds and renowned investment institutions like Haoran Capital and MiraclePlus, helping startup projects connect with funding and resources.

  • Entrepreneurial Mentor Support: Senior entrepreneurial mentor team provides guidance, helping define users and needs, and offering advice on market, financing, technology, team, and other aspects.

  • Incubator and Accelerator Matching: Providing incubator and accelerator resource matching, helping obtain talent subsidies, office space, market promotion, technical support, and other necessary resources to promote rapid project development.
