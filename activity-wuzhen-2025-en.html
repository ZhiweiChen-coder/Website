5th 'Wuzhen Cup' High-level Talent Entrepreneurship Innovation Competition Finals — Boundless Youth Forum Member Yang Di Wins Third Prize

Date: December 15, 2025 Location: Wuzhen, Zhejiang Tags: #Wuzhen Cup #Competition #AI Application #Third Prize

Event Summary

Congratulations to Yang Di, member of Boundless Youth Forum and founder of SkyVeil Jewelry AI, for winning third prize in the 5th "Wuzhen Cup" High-level Talent Entrepreneurship Innovation Competition Finals. This is an important milestone for him and his team, and a vivid practice of the forum's "Connect, Empower, Co-create" philosophy.

The Wuzhen Cup is renowned for its rigorous evaluation system and high-standard industry resources, selecting quality projects in key tracks such as artificial intelligence, advanced manufacturing, and life sciences, promoting the transformation of technological achievements from laboratory to industrialization. During the competition, Yang Di engaged in multiple rounds of in-depth exchanges with judges, industry mentors, and investment institutions, receiving high recognition in terms of technical innovation, business model feasibility, and implementation path clarity.

As an enabler and connector, Boundless Youth Forum provides integrated support for participating members from material preparation and roadshow coaching to investment and financing connections. We will continue to work with local government talent introduction programs and industrial park resources to help more youth teams shine on bigger stages.

The competition focused on cutting-edge technology and industry implementation, covering tracks such as AI applications, intelligent manufacturing, and life sciences, attracting excellent teams from home and abroad to compete on the same stage. The event provides winning projects with funding support of 2 million yuan or more, landing resources, and investment and financing connections for full-chain support, providing valuable platforms and resource support for young entrepreneurs.
